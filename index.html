UIUC CS 416 - Narrative Visualization - The Covid-19 cases in New York

By Haiyue Zhang

My narrative visualization project displays the line chart of Covid-19 cases in New York from 2021-01-23 to 2022-02-23. This period exhibits significant fluctuations, which allows us to observe the trends and changes during this period.

Here are five scenes. To be specific, four of them represent different time intervals, each showcasing distinct trend types which are Recession, Plateau, Fluctuating, and Surge, Plummeting. Also, the fifth scene provides an overview of the entire period.
